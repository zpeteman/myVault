## German W-Questions (W-Fragen)

- **Wer** — *Who*  
  **Use:** asks about a person (subject)
- **Was** — *What*  
  **Use:** asks about a thing, idea, or action
- **Wann** — *When*  
  **Use:** asks about time or moment
- **Wo** — *Where*  
  **Use:** asks about a fixed location
- **Wohin** — *Where to*  
  **Use:** asks about direction or movement toward a place
- **Woher** — *Where from*  
  **Use:** asks about origin or source
- **Warum** — *Why*  
  **Use:** asks for reason or cause
- **Wie** — *How*  
  **Use:** asks about manner, method, or condition
- **Wie viel** — *How much*  
  **Use:** asks about quantity (uncountable)
- **Wie viele** — *How many*  
  **Use:** asks about quantity (countable)
- **Wie lange** — *How long*  
  **Use:** asks about duration
- **Wie oft** — *How often*  
  **Use:** asks about frequency
- **Welcher / Welche / Welches** — *Which*  
  **Use:** asks to choose between options (gendered)
- **Wessen** — *Whose*  
  **Use:** asks about possession or ownership

## Sätze
